a bunch of sites i've encountered

cool sites i've discovered on neocities
  • the old net - an internet archive kinda thing
  • museum of web art - a museum that focuses on the art, technology, and culture of the world wide web
  • web design museum - a museum that focuses on web design from 1991 to 2006 (basically before the internet got too corporate and boring)
  • monoskop - a wiki for arts, media, and humanities
  • brutalist websites - a site that showcases websites that break the status quo of modern and contemporary web design
  • hoverstat.es - another site that showcases experimental websites
  • tim rodenbroker - empowers young creatives by teaching essential skills in the intersection of art, design, and technology
  • glass animals - a psychedelic pop band that focuses heavily on 90's nostalgia (i've yet to listen to their newest album, but i'm sure it'll be worth listening to, the site also looks really cool and i want to achieve a similar thing with mine at some point)
  • cameron's world - an actual masterpiece that acts as an archive for old geocities sites
  • liooil - an interdisciplinary vr artist with an epic site
  • off site project - an internet art kinda thing with some exhibitions
  • lattice.tools - artist's website with an epic web design that looks like a site straight from the 90's
  • joe mygan - a really cool interdisciplinary artist who explores the inner depths of consciousness through sound
  • vern's world - an underground artist that crosses over between diy noise and edm
  • eboy - a pixel art group who create really complex illustrations
